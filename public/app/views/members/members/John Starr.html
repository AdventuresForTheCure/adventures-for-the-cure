Since getting my first Schwinn Sting Ray when I was 7, I was hooked on bikes and never looked back. Biking has connected me to a healthful lifestyle and a fantastic community of people who have become some of my closest friends.

I love all types of riding, but my passion is mtn biking. I have been mtn biking for the last 20 years and racing part time for almost as long. Recently, I have started to enjoy riding greater distances and look forward to completing more endurance events with AFC this year.
