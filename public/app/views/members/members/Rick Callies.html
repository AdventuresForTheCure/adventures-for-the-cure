As Wendy and I were preparing to move here from Durango, Colorado, everyone kept asking "Why?". "It is the Moab of the East" I replied. And as Wendy and I explore the trails here we have come to realize that the trails are nothing like Moab-more like Durango, and in some ways-better! If only they would cut the dang trees OUT...Patapsco is amazing, the road riding is hilly, and the people share our passion for cycling! And the weather isn't so bad...yet.
