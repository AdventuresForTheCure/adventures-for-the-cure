In 1995, after moving to MD, I started riding/training with a good friend who use to road race.  I did a couple bike tours (Cycle Across MD and Bike Virginia) then started racing mt. bikes. 
Six months after my 4th child was born I decided to sign up for my first triathlon (2008 Columbia Tri) and was hooked.  Since then I've raced several sprint and Olympic distance tris along with an half Ironman as part of a relay team.    My favorite racing is off road anything!

Joining AFC has helped to give my dabbling into racing a purpose. Having had a father-in-law (type 2) and brother-in-law (type 1) suffer from diabetes and succumb to all it's complications I thought...."I need to help with awareness of this disease."  Adventures For the Cure helps my family and I give back to our community and add a little hope to the world. :)
