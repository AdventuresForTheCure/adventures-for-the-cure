Andy has enjoyed outdoor sports since his teens when surfing and rock climbing ruled his life. Since then he has pursued triathlon, stand-up paddling, and trail running. Some recurring injuries during training for an ultra marathon led him to purchase a road bike and tune-up his mountain bike as a way to stay in shape. Well, one thing led to another and now he is obsessed with cycling and mountain biking, having re-vamped his life to accommodate his desire to be on the bike. AFC and their mission is a perfect fit for Andy, as he is a congenital heart patient, having undergone several extensive heart surgeries throughout his life. He is excited to spread the word of AFC in his native Northeast Pennsylvania and contribute to raising awareness and support for people with chronic diseases.
