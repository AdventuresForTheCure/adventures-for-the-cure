Ken is most often found in the woods of Patapsco on his rigid single speed, but also enjoys long road rides and mixed-surface rides and loves a good adventure ride. He proudest recent accomplishment is finishing the 2012 SM100 despite rain and miles and miles of mud. He also works closely with MORE-MTB.org in maintaining and building local trails. Ken is looking forward to competing in the occasional race and helping spread the positive message of AFC.
