Christian, Husband, Father, Son, Brother, Dog Owner, Biker and I work! That's me for the ADD - in order of priorities.

I was a national level swimmer and competed through college and beyond. Got into some open ocean swims while living in Hawai'i. Loved it until I fell ill with pneumonia in 2009 which, in turn attacked the nerves that make my shoulder work. Now, it doesn't work so well. When this happened I had to find another outlet for a highly competitive and active personality, and biking was a natural fit.

First keep coming - just competed in my first cyclocross race at Rockburn Cross with The Hub - think I liked it the first race. 2 Races in one day - not so much fun.

Currently I am working with Scott Westcoat and Chris Adair at The Hub Bike Shop in Catonsville and holding down my own business designing, building, maintaining and marketing Web Sites for small businesses while balancing in a Home, 2 wonderful kids and truly the best Wife in the World.

I have been but working out with AFC members for months at Westside Worlds, TMR and The Hub-A- Round rides. It seems only natural to be a part of a fantastic organization like AFC that puts individual accomplishment to better use. Especially because my individual accomplishments have totaled me a whopping $12 in cash and on bag of swag, so - helping out with causes bigger than the individual makes the world a little more tolerable.

Looking forward to helping out where I can, and I'll see you at The Hub and C'Ville Bikes as well as on the road (and trails??)!
