I enjoy being able to push myself to get better on the bike, and am constantly looking for ways to improve. I am looking forward to getting faster and riding further.

I first learned of Adventures for the Cure through the Hub and at the Westside Worlds group ride. What I've found is that AFC is a great club with lots of talented riders, who are also passionate about helping out others and making a difference. I am hoping to push my own limits through AFC, and help promote the team and their message by doing what I can to help out.
