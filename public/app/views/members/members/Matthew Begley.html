I genuinely love all activities outdoors, I'm a water boy at heart and surfing is probably my number one passion.  I ran Track for the University of New Hampshire; my events being the Polevault and 4x400m relay.  Post graduation I found myself missing the competitiveness and camaraderie I had at UNH.  I started cycling to stay in shape and attempt to keep my surfing bug "at bay" between swells and the flat East Coast summers.  Soon after I was linked up with a few AFC'ers and haven't looked back since joining the team in 2010.  I primarily ride road, sprinting being my strength but have dabbled with mountain bike and cyclocross.  I look forward to training and competing on a more serious level over the next couple years; along with traveling for some mountain bike camping trips!   AFC is a great team with a place for everyone, I look forward to every Adventure!
