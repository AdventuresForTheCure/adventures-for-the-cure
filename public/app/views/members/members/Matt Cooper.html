Matt has been a life-long mountain biker and decided to join the AFC team in 2011 after picking up a road bike the previous season, realizing just how addicting the thrill of the road can be. He enjoys the challenge of pushing himself to his limits and hopes to become competitive as he progresses in the sport. Matt also enjoys rock climbing and teaching his two young daughters the enjoyment of the outdoors. Matt is excited about being a part of a team and looks forward to learning from experienced and established riders.
