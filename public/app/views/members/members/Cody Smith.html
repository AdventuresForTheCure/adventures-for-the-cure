I started running and weightlifting my junior year of college to get my weight under control after ignoring my health since high school sports. After hitting my limit on the treadmill, I took my training outside. Shortly after, I found myself participating in a few duathlons, half-marathons, and the Baltimore Marathon. In 2010 I graduated from the University of Maryland and took a year off from training to focus on the establishment of my career as a software engineer. During this time I added swimming to my work out routine.

I'm now back to training for competitions and eager to start triathlons. Previously I had only trained for running and just "did" the biking at race time. After struggling a few weeks through WsW, I found the collection of riders and stories to be a true inspiration.

When I'm not training I'm a technology geek, sports fan and action sports enthusiast (adrenaline junkie) always up to challenging myself with something new.
