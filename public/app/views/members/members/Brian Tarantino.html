I converted from a lifelong soccer player to cyclist at the end of 2010 after three orthopedists gave me the same direction: "stop running". (bad knees). A good friend of mine introduced me to mountain biking and I've been obsessed ever since: a great workout, adrenaline pumping downhills, massive climbs and little to no impact on my knees. Biking has literally changed my life.

I started road biking as well in 2011 and then competing in 2012. I particularly enjoy XC racing and the road bike portion of team duathlons/triathlons.

My favorite rides are the trails in Patapsco State Park and the roads through western Howard County. When I'm not biking, I can be found playing the guitar. My day job is a structural engineer for an airport design firm.

I'm looking forward to training, racing and volunteering with the team in the future.
