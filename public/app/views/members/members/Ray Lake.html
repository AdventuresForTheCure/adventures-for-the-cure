Member of TMR

Ray has participated in numerous running events, triathlons and duathlons. With knee surgeries in 2006 and 2010, Ray bought a road bike in 2009 and has become an avid cyclist. He has completed the Seagull (2009) and Civil War (2010) Centuries. He enjoys the camaraderie of participating in TMR and BDR rides. Last year as the cyclist in a co-ed relay in the Columbia Triathlon, he set a PR for the bike course and his relay team placed second in that division. Ray is planning to do more running races, triathlon relays , another century and perhaps a cyclocross race in 2011.

He wants to spread the word about a relatively unknown condition he has called blepharospasm which manifested in 2007. This is where the ocular muscles spasm at the slightest irritant such as wind, sun, cold and dryness. Ray has adapted to the condition by wearing special cycling glasses with foam inserts around the lenses. Presently, there is no cure for blepharospasm but there are symptomatic treatments.
