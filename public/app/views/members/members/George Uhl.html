CAT 3 Cyclocross
Prodigal Member of TMR
 

George is a Masters 55+ Cat 3 cyclocross racer. George started cycling in 2006 after his doctor warned him about the dangers of living a sedentary lifestyle in middle age. An avid competive distance runner in the 1980's, George succumbed to injuries that forced him to retire from racing. Two decades and 50 pounds later he rediscovered that long lost feeling of satisfaction from a good aerobic workout after a bike ride with his wife on the BWI trail. He started riding on TheMorningRide and quickly became a regular on the group rides. It was only a matter of time before his competitive juices would reawaken and he would start racing bikes as well. He began cyclocross in the Fall of 2010.

George's wife is a Type I diabetic, his father was and is brother is a Type II so he has a personal stake in the mission to find a cure. He finds the dedication of the AFC community and their committment to help defeat diabetes inspiring and a source of hope that a cure will be found in his lifetime.
