Cat 5 Road
Cat 3 Cross

2012 was my first season really racing. I did a handful of road races and planned on trying "1 or 2" cyclocross races that Fall. I started out at Tachino CX and upon finishing I immediately thought 2 things: 1) That was the hardest thing I've ever done. 2) When can I do it again. I ended up racing 12 or so cross races that Fall and became totally hooked. I was pleasantly surprised when by the end of the season I was doing well enough to podium and even win a race. I was able to upgrade to Cat 3 towards the end of the season and I look forward to the challenge that presents in 2013.
