Chris has been diabetic since the age of 31. He has always led an active, athletic lifestyle. While attending the University of Maryland Baltimore County he was a pole vaulter with the men's Track & Field team. He also occasionally competed in the hurdles and javelin throw. He graduated from UMBC in 1999 with a degree in biological sciences. After graduation he was employed as a researcher, diagnostic equipment marketing representative, and a pharmaceutical regulatory affairs project manager, where he is currently employed with the Food & Drug Administration.
 
Chris is married to Randi Korenman Hilfiger and the couple has a daughter. They reside in EllicottCity, MD.
Chris is a Cat 5 road racer and Cat 3 cyclocross racer.
