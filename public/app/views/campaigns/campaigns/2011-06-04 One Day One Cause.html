One Day One Cause Campaign

AFC team member, Chris Lewis, rode 60 miles of trail in Patapsco State Park on Friday, June 3rd (original date was rained out) to raise money for DESTINY's Extreme Weekend For Children With Diabetes. 100% of donations went towards helping children attend the camp who could not afford it otherwise. His day started at five thirty in the morning at the Avalon area across from the park and ride and ended after more than sixty miles of trail throughtout the park. He actually used the AFC Sugar Hill Expert Mountain bike course for 99% of the ride! That is a very challenging course!
