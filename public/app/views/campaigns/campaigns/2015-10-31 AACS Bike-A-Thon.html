AACS Soccer Bike-A-Thon

Annapolis Area Christian School is at it again! AACS will be cruising the BWI trail in hopes of raising money for Kupenda for the Children.

  • Start Location Friendship Park on the BWI trail (the plane overlook)
  • Date/Time Saturday, October 31st 10am-2pm

Their goal is to raise $500 to help children with disabilities in Kenya receive food, shelter, care, and education for one term. Due to a local stigma, these children are often mistreated or even killed because they are believed to be cursed by God. With your help, however, you can make a difference in these communities by partnering with AACS to empower the lives of 170 children!

For more information on Kupenda please visit their website at http://kupenda.org.
