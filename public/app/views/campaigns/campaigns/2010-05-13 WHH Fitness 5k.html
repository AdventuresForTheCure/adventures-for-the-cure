SUCCESS!

The WHH Fitness 5k, held on March 13th (post-poned due to rain) successfully raised over $800 for Extreme Weekend For Children With Diabetes!

WHH Fitness WHH Fitness' founder and CEO, Rick Callahan, has dedicated his life to helping people live healthier, happier lives through nutrition and fitness education. He has a personal vendetta against Diabetes Type 1 as his 6 year old daughter is victim to this terrible disease. He would like to raise awareness about this disease as well as inform people that Diabetes Type II, which several of their clients are faced with, is preventable by increasing physical activity and making healthier nutrition choices.
