24 Hour Columbia Gateway Fundraiser - Jesse Tubb

Jesse Tubb's plans to ride the Columbia Gateway loop for 24 hours the last weekend of June with a distance goal of 350ish miles to raise money for OhanaHC, Volunteer, Nonprofit - OhanaHC - Columbia, Maryland

The Details

  • He will be using live tracking on Strava. Once he starts the link will be posted to this webpage.
  • The ride will start on Saturday June 27th at 10:00 am
  • 100% of money made will be sent directly to OhanaHC after donations have stopped.
