TRI it for a cause! Joining Adventures For the Cure and make a difference!
Covering 70.3 miles is no easy task when you are using your own personal power to get to the finish line. Swimming 1.2 miles, biking 56 miles and then running 13.1 can seem overwhelming and extremely challenging. But think about those living with Diabetes and the challenges that they face every day.
On June 14th, 2009, 3 incredibly inspiring people will be competing in a 1/2 ironman triathlon to show those diagnosed with Diabetes and their families how leading a healthy and active lifestyle can move you beyond your perceived limits, and to raise funds for Diabetes research. If you would like to support the cause, please consider sponsoring us for each mile that we cover during the race. You will be helping us to raise awareness and funds for diabetes research, education and advocacy.
Deb - The Swimmer
Debbie Chaney will be participating in the swim leg of the race. She will start off the 70.3 total miles with a 1.2 mile swim in the Choptank River that runs through Cambridge Maryland. Her goal is to stay ahead of the sharks ;-)
Julie - The Cyclist
Julie Boughn is a regional time trial champion cyclist. She will be completing the 56 mile bike leg of the race. She is fast and furious on the bike especially on a flat course.
Mike - The Runner
Mike Keane will complete the half marathon run. Mike has competed in many triathlons and run many races for worthy causes. Look for Mike at your next 10K fun run... most likely he will be there.
