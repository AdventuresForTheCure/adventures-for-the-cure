AFC Everesting

A few members of AFC have decided that they want try an attempt at "Everesting" a local climb. What better hill to do it on than our local Ilchester Road climb or Bonnie Branch / Beachwood Road in Ellicott City MD. Of course we will be doing this for charity... all donations and money made during this event will go to support Kupenda for the Children and FOD Family Support Group.

  • Start Location Bottom of Ilchester Road climb
  • Date Saturday, April 1st
  • Estimated Start Time 6am
  • End Time It should take 12+ hours to finish!
  • Base of operations during the event will be at the bottom of Ilchester Road at the intersection of Bonnie Branch.
