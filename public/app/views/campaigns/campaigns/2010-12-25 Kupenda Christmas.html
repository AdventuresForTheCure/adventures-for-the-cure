Kupenda Christmas 2010!

In lieu of a traditional Christmas gift for friends and family this year, consider giving a gift that will make a difference in someone's life! Purchase a whole or a share of a gift to help children with disabilities in Kenya. All proceeds raised by AFC this Christmas will be given directly to Kupenda for the Children. After purchasing your gift, download a Kupenda Christmas Gift Card or request one via mail in the memo line at checkout.

After making your donation, click on one of the cards below to print it out and send as a gift.

Gifts To Purchase

Physical Therapy
It costs just $100 for a child to receive a year of physical therapy. Children that attend the Gede Special School for handicapped children receive daily physical therapy through Kupenda's general fund. In addition, some children not yet in school, but who are still in need of assistance, also benefit from Kupenda's services. Children who have literally been carried in for therapy are now enjoying the benefit of running.
Total Donated: $823.66 (updated nightly)

Emergency Medical Attention
Children that attend any of the 11 special needs facilities in the Malindi District are often in need of emergency medical attention. For $1500 a year, an emergency medical transportation and medical assistance fund is made available to over 500 children with disabilities. This means emergency medical attention for an average of just $3 per child per year! Some of the emergency needs include malaria treatment, doctor visits for epileptic attacks, shunting, and other services.
Total Donated: $436.25 (updated nightly)

Classroom Furniture
Approximately $200 funds the purchase of furniture for an entire special needs classroom. Many classrooms in the Malindi District of Kenya do not have desks or chairs--even in the classrooms for children without disabilities. Kupenda funds the purchase of regular desks, u-tables for deaf classes, chairs, and other important items for our children.
Total Donated: $213.12 (updated nightly)

Assistance Equipment
Some of the equipment needs of the Kupenda children include crutches, wheelchairs, and leg braces. The price of a wheelchair in Kenya is approximately $350. It is best to send funds rather than sending the items, since shipping overseas can be more costly than simply purchasing the items in Kenya. This also helps to support the local economy.
Total Donated: $433.04 (updated nightly)

Corrective Surgery
Kupenda funds corrective surgeries, which include surgery for children that suffer from contractures (due to cerebral palsy), spina bifida treatment, heart problems associated with Down's syndrome, club foot correction, and other needs of children with disabilities. Kupenda partners with Kijabe Hospital for crippled children in addition to the Association for the Physically Disabled of Kenya (APDK) for medical treatment of special needs children in the Malindi District. With this assistance, the average cost Kupenda expends on corrective surgery is only approximately $850 per child. The cost fluctuates depending on the type of surgery.
Total Donated: $561.99 (updated nightly)

Student/Teacher Supplies
Often, items such as classroom supplies, texts, and teacher materials are needed at the Kupenda-sponsored schools. Giving to this fund would help to adequately supply classrooms. This would greatly enhance the teacher's effectiveness. Monies for student uniforms are also needed.
Total Donated: $96.50 (updated nightly)
