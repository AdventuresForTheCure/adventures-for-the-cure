The Challenge
Kristopher Auer is a fellow cyclist that races on a team that often competes agains Adventures For the Cure at many of the local cycling races, called C3 Solay.com. He told us that he wanted to do a crazy kayak trip to raise money for Adventures For the Cure.
This January \'09 Kris will be kayaking from Everglades City, FL down to Flamingo just off the Florida Keys. He will be traveling through swamp, marsh, mangrove forests and ocean. With abundant wild life including crocodiles, alligators, sharks and dolphins in addition to all types of birds. The trip will be roughly 120 miles, and it will take roughly 7 days. There is no fresh water anywhere in the everglades so he will have to bring everything himself. Tides and wind will be major factors in his trip.
