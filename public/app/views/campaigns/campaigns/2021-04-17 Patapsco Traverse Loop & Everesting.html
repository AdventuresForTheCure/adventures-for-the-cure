Patapsco Traverse Loop & Everesting!

What is this?
  • On Sat Apr 17th, AFC co-founder Patrick Blair will be running the full length of Patapsco State Park out-and-back. This will be about 64miles in total!
    • Following this route: https://ridewithgps.com/routes/35380488
    • Starting at sunrise
    • Anyone who would like to join Patrick for part (or all!) of this run should reach out to adventuresforthecure@gmail.com... the more the merrier!!
  • On Wed Apr 21st, AFC founder Adam Driscoll along with founders of Columbia Gear Closet / AFC members: Dan Frank and Jesse Tubb will be 'Everesting' Ilchester!
    • To 'Everest' is to climb directly up and down a single climb until the total elevation accumulation is that of the height of Mount Everest.
    • Here is the segment that they will be doing repeats on Everesting Ilchester - Strava Segment
    • Anyone who would like to join them to complete this ride should reach out to adventuresforthecure@gmail.com... feel free to join them for all or part of the ride, they will be on the climb for most of the day.

Raising money for Kupenda For the Children, Friends of Patapsco Valley State Park, and Columbia Gear Closet
