In America we are so blessed to have an endless supply of clean drinking water. Other people are not so blessed. I am training for the next half year to accomplish 2 goals. The first is to complete the challenging Great Bay Bridge Swim a 4.4 mile swim across the Chesapeak Bay. The second and more important, as I train for this event, is raising awareness and funds for a much needed cause "Blood:Water Mission".
Blood:Water Mission is a group of individuals committed to building wells to provide clean drinking water in different parts of Africa that need your help. Please visit the Blood:Water Mission website and contribute to this Mission! $1=1 year of clean water for 1 human!
