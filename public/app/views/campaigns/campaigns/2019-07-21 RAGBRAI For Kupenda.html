RAGBRAI for Kupenda 2019

For the second time in three years, Adventures for the Cure and Dan Zavorka are out to conquer RAGBRAI to benefit Kupenda! On Sunday July 21st, the AFC cyclist will begin his seven day ride across the state of Iowa in hopes of raising money for Kupenda for the Children. The 400 plus mile ride will be documented along the way on Instagram under #afc4kupenda.

The goal is to raise $1,000 to help children with disabilities in Kenya receive food, shelter, care, and education. Due to a local stigma, these children are often mistreated or even killed because they are believed to be cursed by God. With your help, however, you can make a difference in these communities by partnering with Dan and AFC to empower the lives of 170 children!

Kupenda for the Children meets this mission through advocacy and sponsorship of school fees, child assistance and medical intervention, construction of special needs facilities, and the coordination of volunteer assistance.
