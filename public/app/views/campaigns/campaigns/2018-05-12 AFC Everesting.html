Everesting Details

A few members of AFC have decided that they want try an attempt at "Everesting" a local climb. What better hill to do it on than our local Ilchester Road climb or Bonnie Branch / Beachwood Road in Ellicott City MD. Of course we will be doing this for charity... all donations and money made during this event will go to support Kupenda for the Children, FOD Family Support Group, and Maryland Interscholasitc Cycling League.

  • Start Location Bottom of Ilchester Road climb
  • Date Saturday, May 12th
  • Estimated Start Time 5am
  • End Time It should take 12+ hours to finish!
  • Base of operations during the event will be at the bottom of Ilchester Road at the intersection of Bonnie Branch.
