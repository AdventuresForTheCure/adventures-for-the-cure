How It Works

Adam, Pat, Jordan, and Lance will be competing in the 2011 MABRA Super8 Cyclocross Series at the Elite level, as well as many other UCI and 'Elite' races throughout the season. All of these races have pretty good payouts if you place high enough.

We do this for the love the the sport! Not to make money. That is what our day jobs are for. We do AFC to make a difference!! Therefore, we thought it would be a great idea to take all of the money we win this year and donate it to AFC. AFC will then take 1/2 of the money we raise and send it to Kupenda For The Children and the other 1/2 will go to DESTINY Diabetes!
