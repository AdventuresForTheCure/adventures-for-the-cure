Patapsco Party 2026 - Dorsey Family Support

The Patapsco Party is about community, adventure, and helping others.

This year’s Patapsco Party is dedicated to supporting the family of one of our own. Darrel Dorsey is an active member of our local ultrarunning community and a Patapsco Party veteran. Earlier this year, his wife, Angela Dorsey, was diagnosed with colon cancer. Her illness has since progressed, and she has recently entered hospice care. This effort is one way our running community is coming together to support the Dorsey family during this incredibly difficult time.

Angela and Darrel have two children, Declan and Lila. All funds raised through this year’s Patapsco Party will go directly toward the Dorsey Family. This is our way of helping provide stability and opportunity in the years ahead, and of showing the strength and love of our community.

We are asking that everyone who participates in the Patapsco Party donate to The Dorsey Family's GoFundMe
