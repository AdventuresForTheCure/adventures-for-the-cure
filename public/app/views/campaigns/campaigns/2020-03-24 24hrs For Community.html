Daniel Frank's children's school normally has a mobile food bank but has been shut down due to the crisis. He wants to try to help in some way by raising money for Community Action Council of Howard County which runs the Food Bank plus provides housing assistance, energy assistance and advocates for low income families. A lot of our low income families are going to be hurt particularly hard by this economic slow down as many are hourly workers who are no longer employed or under employed.

The Plan
Dan Frank will be running for 24 hour to raise money for CAC. He is asking for overall donations, donations per mile, with the option to contribute bonus donation for reaching 100 miles and for any CR's (Strava Course Records aka KOM's in cycling terms) along the way.

The Details

  • He will be using live tracking on Strava. Once he starts the link will be posted to this webpage.
  • Live updates will be posted here 24 Hours For Community - Facebook Group
  • The run will start on Friday April 3rd at 6:00 pm.
  • 100% of money made will be sent directly to CAC after donations have stopped.
