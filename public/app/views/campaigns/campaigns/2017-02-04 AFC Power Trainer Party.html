Event Details

  • Check out all the latest updates and trash talking on the AFC Power Trainer Party Facebook Event Page
  • The event will consists of multiple different competition starting at 8am.
  • Sign up for one or many competitions for a suggested donation of $10 per competition. Cash, check, paypal, and credit card will be accepted at the event.
  • Each winner will go onto the next round and competitions will set up in brackets so we keep things moving very quickly.
  • Kickrs/trainers are NOT needed unless you are do the 1 hour of power.
  • Exact timing of when you will ride will be determined when we get closer to the event.
  • For the 1 and 5 minute tests we will provide a scale at the event that you will use to get your exact weight before performing the test.
  • For the 1 and 5 minute tests we will be using Zwift. It will be a head to head competition.
