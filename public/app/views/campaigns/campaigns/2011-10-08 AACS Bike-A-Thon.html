AACS Soccer Bike-A-Thon

Annapolis Area Christian School is at it again! On Saturday, October 8th, the AACS varsity soccer team and friends will be cruising the BWI trail in hopes of raising money for Kupenda for the Children. Along with being the best soccer players they can be, AACS has a deep desire to serve others and will participate in the 3rd annual AACS soccer bike-a-thon.

The Goal - To Raise $8,500!

Their goal is to raise $8,500 to help children with disabilities in Kenya receive food, shelter, care, and education for one term. Due to a local stigma, these children are often mistreated or even killed because they are believed to be cursed by God. With your help, however, you can make a difference in these communities by partnering with AACS to empower the lives of 170 children!

For more information on Kupenda please visit their website at http://kupenda.org.
