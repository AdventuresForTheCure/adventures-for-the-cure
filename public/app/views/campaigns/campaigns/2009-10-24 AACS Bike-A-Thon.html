AACS Soccer Bike-A-Thon

The members of the 2009-2010 JV soccer team at AACS High School participated in a bike-a-thon on Oct 24, 2009 to raise money for Kupenda For The Children. Kupenda ("to love" in Kiswahili) For The Children is a 501 (c)(3) tax-exempt, Christian-based organization that exists to meet the needs of children with disabilities.

The Goal - SUCCESS!

The goal was to raise the $3,000 needed to pay for the life saving medical expenses for one of the children of Kupenda named Erick. The team ended up raising a grand total of $5,582.64!!! The extra money will also go to Kupenda.
