2014 Miles To The Moon

Kenya is around 8,000 miles away from the United States. Most Americans don't have a lot in common with people that are 8,000 miles away besides their humanity. There is one thing that we all have in common, though. There is a dot in the sky that is 239,000 miles away that we all can see. On any given night one of the children that AFC helps with Kupenda for the Kids can stare at the same moon that lights our path during night time runs and Mountain Bike Rides. It is our goal - as a team - to ride/run/swim/walk/hike/crawl 239,000 miles in 2014.

Team members that wish to participate in the "Miles to the Moon" challenge will donate $10 at the beginning of every month of the year. Using Strava, TrainingPeaks, GarminConnect and other 3rd party training sites we will add up member miles in the hopes of reaching the moon! Upon the successful completion of the "Miles to the Moon" challenge any member that has donated each month will be compensated with a "Miles to the Moon" t-shirt.
