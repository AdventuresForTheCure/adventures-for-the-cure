Rob's Mission

On November 13th, AFC Team Member Rob Irias road 200.58 miles in 24hrs to raise money for Kupenda For The Children.

Kupenda needs to make a certain amount of money every single month in order to support the number of kids that they do. This month they were in serious danger of running short of that goal. This inspired Rob to take action! Pushing his limits he is going to ride further in one day than he ever has before.
