Sugar Hill 2011 - Beginner Course (5.75 miles)


Start at Pavilion
West on river road
Left on Ridge Trail (aka Waterbars)
Right at 4 way intersection on Morning Choice Trail
Stay right on Morning Choice though Old Track Loop area
Cross Norris Lane
Stay right off of Morning Choice down to Cascade Trail
Right on Cascade Trail
Right on Ridge Trail
Stay left on Ridge Trial (aka Waterbars)
Right on River Road to Pavilion
