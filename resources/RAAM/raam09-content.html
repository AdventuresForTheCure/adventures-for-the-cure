Why do we do it?
In June, 2009 Adam and Pat competed in Race Across America as a 2 Man team. We raced on geared (not fixed) bikes! We were successful in winning the race and raising well over $30,000 for Extreme Weekend for Children with Diabetes and Kupenda for the Children. Thank you to everyone who supported our efforts!
